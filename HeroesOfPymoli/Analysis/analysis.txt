# Three observable trends in Heroes of Pymoli purchase data

1) Males make up 84.03% of the sales making them the largest generators of Revenue with more individual purchases than females and although their average purchase price is slightly less than females.

2) Players in the 20-24 age range made the largest percentage of purchases at 44.79% but their average purchase price ($3.05) was neither the greatest ($3.60 for 35-39 year olds) or the least ($2.93 for 30-34 year olds).

3)The top spender paid on average $3.79 per item and bought 5 items. Item 92, the Final Critic was the most bought item (purchased 13 times) making up 2.52% of total revenue.
